# Learnings: Takeaways Writer

## Best Practices

### Takeaway Format
- Bullet points or numbered list
- 3-5 key takeaways per chapter
- Action-oriented language
- Specific and memorable

### Content Selection
- Most important concepts from chapter
- Actions reader can take immediately
- Key phrases to remember
- Mindset shifts emphasized

### "Tomorrow Challenge"
- Include one specific action for tomorrow
- Make it achievable in 24 hours
- Tie it to a key chapter concept
- Make success measurable

## Do's
- Write takeaways that stand alone
- Use strong verbs (Use, Try, Practice, Remember)
- Include the "quick reference" value
- Make them quotable and shareable

## Don'ts
- Don't just summarize the chapter
- Don't include vague advice
- Don't list more than 5 takeaways
- Don't make tomorrow challenge too hard
