# Learnings: Chapter Translator

## Best Practices

### Translation Approach
- Translate meaning, not just words
- Use glossary terms consistently
- Maintain voice and tone
- Preserve emotional impact

### Quality Targets
- Natural-sounding Spanish
- Consistent with style guide
- Glossary compliance 100%
- Length within 10-20% of English

### Special Handling
- Scripts: Keep dialogue natural
- Stories: Adapt cultural references
- Quotes: Verify translation captures meaning
- Technical terms: Use glossary exactly

## Do's
- Read full section before translating
- Use terminology glossary
- Follow Spanish style guide
- Flag unclear source text

## Don'ts
- Don't translate word-by-word
- Don't miss glossary terms
- Don't change meaning
- Don't lose the author's voice
