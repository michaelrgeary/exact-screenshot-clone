# Learnings: Chapter Title Generator

## Best Practices

### Title Qualities
- Intriguing but clear
- Promises value
- Uses action words when possible
- Consistent style across book

### Title Formats
- "How to [Achieve Outcome]"
- "The [Adjective] [Concept]"
- "[Number] Ways to [Achieve Result]"
- Question format that chapter answers

### Roofing-Specific Considerations
- Can include roofing terms if widely understood
- Should resonate with roofing salespeople
- Avoid too technical for the title
- Balance professional and approachable

## Do's
- Capture the chapter's main promise
- Make titles skimmable in table of contents
- Ensure titles don't overlap in meaning
- Test: Would someone click on this?

## Don'ts
- Don't make titles too clever (clarity first)
- Don't use jargon readers might not know
- Don't make titles too long (5-8 words ideal)
- Don't give away everything (create curiosity)
