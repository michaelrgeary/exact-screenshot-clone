# Learnings: Reading Level Analyzer

## Best Practices

### Target Level
- Grade 7-8 reading level
- Accessible to non-native speakers
- Professional but not academic
- Easy to skim and scan

### Metrics to Check
- Average sentence length (15-20 words)
- Syllables per word (aim for 1.5 average)
- Paragraph length (3-5 sentences)
- Technical term density

### Improvement Strategies
- Break long sentences
- Use simpler synonyms
- Explain jargon when first used
- Add white space

## Do's
- Calculate Flesch-Kincaid or similar
- Flag sections significantly above target
- Suggest specific simplifications
- Balance with maintaining expertise

## Don'ts
- Don't dumb down expert content
- Don't sacrifice accuracy for simplicity
- Don't miss sections that are too complex
- Don't over-simplify already clear content
